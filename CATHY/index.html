Top Foods to Try in Switzerland

Fondue

Melted cheese served with bread for dipping. It’s a classic Swiss dish, perfect for sharing.

Rösti

Crispy potato dish, often served as a side. It’s similar to hash browns and can be topped with cheese, eggs, or vegetables.

Älplermagronen

A hearty pasta dish with potatoes, cream, cheese, and sometimes onions or apples. It’s comfort food at its best!

Schnitzel

Breaded and fried meat, typically served with a lemon wedge and sides like potatoes or salad.
