Learn More about Interlaken

Interlaken is a stunning alpine town in Switzerland, surrounded by towering mountains and picturesque villages. It's a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with a wide range of activities and attractions to explore.

Things to Do in Interlaken

  • Hiking and trekking in the surrounding mountains
  • Water sports on Lake Thun and Lake Brienz
  • Visiting the Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe"
  • Exploring the picturesque villages of Lauterbrunnen and Grindelwald
  • Adventure sports like paragliding, skydiving, and canyoning

Must-see Attractions

  • The Jungfraujoch Railway
  • The Schilthorn Mountain
  • The Giessbach Waterfalls
  • The St. Beatus Caves
  • The picturesque villages of Lauterbrunnen and Grindelwald

Accommodation and Travel Information

Interlaken has a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. You can get to Interlaken by train or car, and there are plenty of travel tips and advice available online.
